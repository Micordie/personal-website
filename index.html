About Me

At the beginning of the Corona Virus pandemic, I started my foray into the world of code. After I felt confident with Python, I decided I wanted to try out something I had been interested in for a long time: Game Design.

After some time working with the Unity Engine and C#, I realized I had no ability to create game assets of my own. So, I began working with Blender, which created a whole new passion for art --some of which can be seen in my gallery! Then I was off to college. This is where my formal Computer Science education started. I am currently studying Computer Science and Game Developement at the University of Pittsburgh, and will be continuing to hone my skills in the years to come.
